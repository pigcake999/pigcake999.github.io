Shimasu

Description

Enhance your learning experience & capacity with YouTube and stop searching for word meanings, definitions, images with Shimasu.

Enhance your learning experience & capacity on YouTube and stop searching for word meanings, definitions, and images with Shimasu. The Shimasu Chrome extension allows you to learn from YouTube videos efficiently and fast by delivering you suggestions based on the content you are watching. It finds the most accurate suggestions to words and concepts which it thinks are possibly difficult for you. Shimasu uses a machine learning algorithm to learn from the way you learn and stack the suggestions accordingly. In simple terms, you now have a personal tutor. Stop searching for word meanings, images or any other content while watching a YouTube video, because Shimasu provides it all right on your screen.

We are currently in beta, so there may be bugs. Make sure to sign up here.
